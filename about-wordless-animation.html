What is Wordless Animation?

Wordless animation is storytelling without dialogue. It lets viewers relax, focus, and feel the atmosphere without language barriers.

Why do creators choose “wordless”?

  • Emotion first: facial expressions, timing, and movement carry the story.
  • Universal viewing: no subtitles needed, easier to share globally.
  • Atmosphere matters: silence can feel calm, intimate, or quietly unsettling.

Why Moriris is wordless

Moriris is designed to be watched quietly. Without dialogue, the animation leaves space for interpretation, emotion, and a subtle sense of unease.
