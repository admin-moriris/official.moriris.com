What are Moriris videos?

Moriris creates long-form, wordless forest videos designed for sleep, focus, waiting time, and quiet moments. They are gentle environments to stay with — not stories you have to follow.

What makes these videos different?

  • Purpose-based: made for sleep, focus, and calm time — not for active viewing.
  • Gentle pacing: subtle movement and slow time that won’t demand attention.
  • Wordless & calm: no dialogue, minimal cues — easier to keep on in the background.

Why Moriris is wordless

Moriris is designed to be watched quietly. Without dialogue, the videos leave space for interpretation and rest — while keeping a calm atmosphere with a slight hint of something unusual.

How to use Moriris

Start with the short introduction video on the official site. Choose a video based on your purpose — sleep, focus, waiting time, or quiet moments — and play it at a comfortable volume.
