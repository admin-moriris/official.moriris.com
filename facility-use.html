Facility Use (Business)

Moriris offers gentle, wordless forest ambience videos designed for calm spaces such as nursery schools and waiting rooms.

How to start

  1. Access the member site and register with your email address.
  2. You will start on the Free plan. Upgrade to Standard after logging in if needed.
  3. Log in anytime, select a video on the member site, and play it on your facility screen.
