Typical use cases in facilities

Developmental Support Centers Used during calm-down or transition time. Wordless visuals help reduce overstimulation.
Medical Waiting Rooms Helps waiting feel calmer without drawing strong attention.
Quiet Rooms / Sensory-Friendly Spaces Functions as a gentle background rather than something to watch.

Design principles

  • Seamless loop: Designed for long, repeated playback.
  • Low stimulation: Small movements, no narration, no sudden changes.
  • Ad-free: No interruptions or unintended content.
